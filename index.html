🧠 Knowledge & Tech

  • CS50 by Harvard: The ultimate free computer science course.
  • FreeCodeCamp: Learn to code for free (Web Dev, Data Science).
  • Google/Meta Certificates: Coursera courses on UX, IT, Data Analytics.
  • Prompt Engineering: Learn how to talk to AI (ChatGPT, Claude).

🗣️ Soft Skills & Mindset

  • Read "Atomic Habits": Build systems, not goals.
  • Stoicism: Read "Meditations" by Marcus Aurelius. Control what you can.
  • Never Split the Difference: Master negotiation in life and salary.
  • Public Speaking: Toastmasters or YouTube channels like Charisma on Command.

💼 Wealth & Investing

  • The 50/30/20 Rule: 50% Needs, 30% Wants, 20% Investing/Saving.
  • Compound Interest: Start investing at 20. Time is your biggest asset.
  • S&P 500 ETFs: (e.g. VUAA, CSPX). Don't pick single stocks, buy the market.
  • Emergency Fund: Save 3-6 months of living expenses in cash.

🏃‍♂️ Health & Biohacking

  • Sleep is King: 7-8 hours minimum. Cold room, no screens before bed.
  • Lift Weights: Build muscle mass early. It protects your joints and metabolism.
  • Nutrition: High protein, low processed sugar. Drink 3L of water.
  • Mental Health: Daily walks without music/podcasts. Let your brain reset.
